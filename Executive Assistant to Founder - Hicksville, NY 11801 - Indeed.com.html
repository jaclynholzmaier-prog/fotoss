PERSONAL ASSISTANT for founder of Fast growing start-up

We have been in business for 5 years and provide industry leading digital marketing and software solutions for our clients. We are not a large corporation, so we offer some flexibility to our staff.

This position is in office, 30-40 hours.

Our office is very close to the Hicksville train station.

Responsibilities:

Assist very active and energetic founder with both business and personal needs - including, but not limited to:
